Back Pain Management: When to See a Specialist

Understanding the causes and when to seek help.

Types of Back Pain and When to Seek Help

Back pain can arise from strained muscles, herniated discs, spinal stenosis, or nerve irritation. Differentiating mechanical discomfort from neuropathic pain is essential for effective management.

Conservative Care

Most patients improve with physiotherapy, activity modification, and pain medications. Injections such as epidurals may provide targeted relief.

Warning Signs

  • Sudden weakness in the legs
  • Loss of bladder or bowel control
  • Pain following a traumatic injury

These symptoms warrant prompt evaluation and imaging studies.
