Physical Therapy: Your Roadmap to Recovery

Essential exercises and tips for post‑surgical rehabilitation.

Stages of Physical Therapy

Recovery begins with gentle passive movements to maintain flexibility, progressing to active exercises that build strength and balance. Your therapist will tailor each phase to your surgery and progress level.

Exercise Examples

  • Range‑of‑motion stretches
  • Resistance band leg lifts and squats
  • Balance drills using a wobble board or single‑leg stance

By adhering to a home program and attending regular sessions, most patients regain independence within weeks to months.
