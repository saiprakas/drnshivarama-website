Nutrition for Joint Health: Foods That Heal

Diet plays a pivotal role in reducing inflammation and supporting tissue repair. A balanced, anti‑inflammatory diet can ease joint pain and enhance recovery.

Key Nutrients

  • Omega‑3 fatty acids from fish, flaxseeds, and walnuts
  • Calcium and vitamin D for bone strength
  • Antioxidant-rich fruits and vegetables
  • Limiting processed sugars and trans fats

Staying hydrated and maintaining a healthy weight further reduces stress on joints.
