Managing Post-Surgery Pain Without Opioids

Modern multimodal pain management approaches.

Safer Pain Management Strategies

To minimize the risk of dependency, many patients now receive multimodal analgesia that combines non‑opioid medications with physical therapies and lifestyle techniques.

Non‑Opioid Options

  • NSAIDs or acetaminophen for mild discomfort
  • Topical treatments and nerve blocks for localized pain
  • Cold therapy and compression to control swelling

Incorporating relaxation, guided imagery, or mindfulness can also reduce perceived pain levels.
