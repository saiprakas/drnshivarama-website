Exercise and Joint Health: Finding the Right Balance

Guidance on safe exercise for joint strength.

Safe Exercise for Strong Joints

Regular movement is vital for joint lubrication and muscle support. Low‑impact activities such as swimming, cycling, or brisk walking are ideal for people of all ages.

Warm‑Up & Cool‑Down

Start each session with gentle stretching to increase blood flow and end with cooldown movements to reduce stiffness.

Listening to Your Body

Avoid pushing through sharp pain; mild discomfort is normal, but any swelling or persistent ache should prompt rest or a consult with your therapist.
