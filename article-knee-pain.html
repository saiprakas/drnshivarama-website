Understanding Knee Pain: Causes, Treatments, and Prevention Strategies

In-depth guide by DR N Shiva Rami Reddy explaining knee pain.

Common Causes of Knee Pain

Knee pain affects millions of people and can result from a variety of conditions such as osteoarthritis, injuries to ligaments or menisci, tendinitis, and overuse. Symptoms may include swelling, stiffness, and difficulty bearing weight.

Diagnosis & Early Care

A thorough evaluation by a specialist includes a physical exam, imaging studies like X‑rays or MRI, and sometimes lab tests. Early conservative measures often involve rest, ice, compression, elevation (RICE), and anti-inflammatory medications.

Treatment Strategies

  • Physical therapy to strengthen surrounding muscles
  • Weight management to reduce joint stress
  • Bracing or orthotics for alignment
  • Minimally invasive procedures such as injections or arthroscopy

When to See a Surgeon

If pain persists despite nonsurgical care or if there is significant instability or deformity, surgical options like partial or total knee replacement may be discussed.
