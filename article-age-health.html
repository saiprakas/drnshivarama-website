Age and Orthopedic Health: What Changes with Time

How joints change and what you can do about it.

How Joints Change with Age

Over time, cartilage thins and joint fluid decreases, which can lead to stiffness and osteoarthritis. Bone density may also decline, increasing fracture risk.

Proactive Measures

  • Maintain a diet rich in calcium, vitamin D, and omega‑3s
  • Engage in regular, low‑impact exercise to keep joints mobile
  • Consider supplements like glucosamine or consult your doctor

Annual check‑ups and early intervention can help manage symptoms and preserve quality of life.
